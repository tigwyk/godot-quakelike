* Play Information *

- Make sure you have the Quake III: Arena point release installed.
  For more information, visit http://www.quake3arena.com/
- Unzip the contents of ztn3dm1.zip to your Quake3\baseq3 folder
- Run Quake III: Arena 
- From the main menu select Single player then Skirmish. Both of the
  maps should appear somewhere on the list, usually at the end.
